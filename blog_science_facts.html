10 Fun Science Facts

  • Octopuses have three hearts.
  • Bananas are berries, but strawberries aren't.
  • Water can boil and freeze at the same time (look up the triple point).
  • Your body has enough iron to make a nail.
  • Sharks are older than dinosaurs.
  • Venus spins backwards.
  • Hot water freezes faster than cold water (the Mpemba effect).
  • Sound travels 4x faster in water than in air.
  • Sloths can hold their breath longer than dolphins.
  • Lightning is five times hotter than the sun.
