Top 5 Study Tips for Students

  1. Use Pomodoro Technique: Study for 25 minutes, take a 5-minute break.
  2. Summarize Notes: After class, write down what you remember in your own words.
  3. Use Flashcards: Great for memorizing facts, especially for science and history.
  4. Teach Someone Else: Explaining helps reinforce your own understanding.
  5. Stay Consistent: Study a little every day rather than cramming before exams.
