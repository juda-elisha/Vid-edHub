Exam Prep: How to Study Without Stress

  1. Create a Plan: Start studying a week early with a schedule.
  2. Find Your Spot: Study in a quiet, organized space.
  3. Practice Tests: Take old tests or quiz yourself.
  4. Stay Active: Don’t sit for too long — move around a bit!
  5. Sleep & Eat Well: Rest your brain and fuel it right.
