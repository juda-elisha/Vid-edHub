How to Build Good Habits for School

Building habits helps students become consistent and successful. Here's how:

  1. Start small: Try 5 minutes of review each day.
  2. Be consistent: Do it at the same time daily.
  3. Track it: Use a habit tracker or checklist.
  4. Reward yourself: After a full week, take a break or treat yourself.
